Deconstruction Rules

What is Deconstruction?

Deconstruction is a game about unbuilding things. At the start of the game, you have a machine that produces widgets. Your goal is to dismantle and sell that machine as cost effectively as possible. Unfortunately, nobody wants these widgets, so the more you produce, the costlier it will be to dispose of them. You dismanlte your machine by assigning your workers to tasks each day. As your workers get better, they can remove the larger pieces of your machine. Each piece of your machine will earn you money on the resale market. Dismantle and sell your machine's parts quickly and cheaply, as the player with the most money at the end of the game wins.
